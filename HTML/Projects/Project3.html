What is Competitive Programming?

Given a problem statement a person must use their knowledge of coding to solve real world problems. People are usually given input and within their code they have to match output.

Who can compete in these competitions?

The contest that I have competed in only have college students competing in them but this is a e-sport that is globally known to programmers. And some of these questions can come up in jobs for interview questions.

When did you start competitive programming?

I started competitive programming around 2019 and is also the time where I won my first programming competition in division 2.

Why is this considered important to you?

Solving these problems in programming helps out my coding skills a lot, I need to know Data Structures and Algorithms to solve these types of problems.

Did you stop practicing problems ever since you won?

No I believe that a person always can learn something new in the world of computer science which is why I looked into other websites that also do competitive programming.
